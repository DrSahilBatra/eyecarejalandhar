Dr Nalini Saxena - Eye Specialist in Jalandhar



Dr. Nalini Saxena specializes in Vitreo - Retinal Surgery and has been exclusively trained in different retinal surgeries. She has done her MS Ophthalmology from Maulana Azad Medical College which is one of the best medical institute of the country. To continue her interest in the retina, she further did a dedicated fellowship in vitreo-retinal Diseases  Management from Venu Eye Institute and Research Center, New Delhi. She has also passed the prestigious examination of the Royal College of Surgeons. Dr. Nalini Saxena is well - versed in the treatment of all medical and surgical diseases of retina she is also a Cataract Surgeon which is the basic eye surgery required in all old age patients.  She has also been in Germany for 2 months for extended training in Complex Vitreo - Retinal surgeries and Cataract Surgeries.

Dr. Nalini Saxena is currently working as an eye specialist in Tagore Hospital, Jalandhar where advanced treatment for all retinal diseases is available. With the goal of providing excellent health care to all patients, Dr. Nalini and her staff are dedicated to providing expert opinion and prudent solutions.  A compassionate physician and surgeon. The power of healing is in the hands of god, but a doctor creates a pathway to better health. Working on the same principle, our doctor has full empathy to all patients. She has academically worked hard to achieve this goal. She has several research publications in national and international recognised journals. She has also participated actively in many Indian and international  conferences(World Ophthalmology Congress, EURETINA).
